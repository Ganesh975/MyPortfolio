MULTI PURPOSE CHATBOT

Introducing my advanced chatbot that makes significant advancements in ChatGPT and Gemini AI! This Multi Purpose Chatbot is designed to offer a wide range of cutting-edge capabilities, making it a versatile tool for various applications.

Advanced Features

My chatbot comes with some cutting-edge capabilities, including:

  • Image Processing: Analyze images and provide detailed information.
  • Object Detection: Identify objects within images.
  • Visual Question Answering: Answer questions about images in multiple languages.
  • Voice Commands: Follow voice commands in different languages.
  • Multilingual Question Answering: Ask and answer questions in multiple languages.
  • Speak Out Options: Read the responses aloud.

This project demonstrates the integration of advanced artificial intelligence technologies to create a multifunctional chatbot that can process and interpret both visual and auditory data. By leveraging image processing, object detection, and natural language processing, the chatbot can understand and respond to a wide variety of inputs, making it an invaluable tool for users across different languages and regions.
