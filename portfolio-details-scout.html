The Bharat Scouts & Guides Achievement

The Bharat Scouts & Guides, District Association, East Godavari, Kakinada
"Be Prepared"

Merit Certificate

This merit certificate is awarded to Yarrampati Ganesh for actively participating and excelling in the AP Model Patrol Leader Training Camp held from January 3, 2018 to January 7, 2018.

Participation Details

Yarrampati Ganesh, a dedicated Scout from Loyala English Medium High School, Hamsavaram, Tuni, successfully attended the AP Model Patrol Leader Training Camp. The training camp was organized by The Bharat Scouts & Guides, District Association, East Godavari, Kakinada.

Role and Responsibilities

During the training camp, I led my team members, demonstrating exceptional leadership skills and teamwork. The camp provided comprehensive training on various survival techniques and scouting skills.

Skills and Techniques Learned

  • Navigation and Orienteering: Learned to use maps and compasses to navigate through unfamiliar terrain.
  • First Aid: Gained knowledge in providing basic first aid in emergency situations.
  • Fire Building: Mastered the skills of building and maintaining a fire in various conditions.
  • Camp Setup: Learned to set up a camp efficiently, including pitching tents and creating safe and comfortable living spaces.
  • Team Leadership: Developed leadership skills by guiding and coordinating team activities, ensuring effective communication and collaboration.
  • Problem-Solving: Enhanced problem-solving abilities through practical challenges and activities designed to simulate real-life survival scenarios.

Impact of the Training

The training camp was a transformative experience, instilling a strong sense of preparedness and resilience. The skills acquired during the camp are invaluable for both scouting activities and real-life situations. Leading my team and learning essential survival techniques have significantly contributed to my personal growth and development as a responsible and resourceful individual.

Certification

This merit certificate is proudly issued by:

  • Scout Master/Guide Captain
  • Leader of the Course Camp Officer
  • District Secretary

The Bharat Scouts & Guides, District Association, East Godavari, Kakinada, recognizes the dedication and achievements of Yarrampati Ganesh in successfully completing the AP Model Patrol Leader Training Camp. This certificate attests to the exceptional skills, commendable performance, and exemplary behavior demonstrated during the training period.
